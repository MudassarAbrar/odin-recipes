Creamy Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Home

Discription

Silky garlic cream sauce coats tender shrimp and al dente pasta. A weeknight favorite with a touch of luxury.

Ingredients

  • pasta (spaghetti or fettuccine)
  • shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • garlic, minced
  • butter
  • cream or half-and-half
  • parmesan cheese, grated
  • salt and pepper
  • parsley, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. Sauté garlic in butter over medium heat until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink and opaque, 2–3 minutes. Remove shrimp and set aside.
  4. Pour in cream, bring to a gentle simmer, and stir in parmesan until melted.
  5. Return shrimp to the pan. If the sauce is too thick, loosen with reserved pasta water.
  6. Toss pasta with the sauce and shrimp. Season with salt, pepper, and parsley to taste.
  7. Serve immediately with extra parmesan if desired.
