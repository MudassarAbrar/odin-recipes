Lemon Herb Chicken with Roasted Vegetables

Home

Discription

Juicy lemon-herb chicken baked with a colorful array of roasted vegetables. Bright, fresh, and comforting in one pan.

Ingredients

  • chicken thighs or breasts
  • lemon, zest and juice
  • olive oil
  • mixed vegetables (carrot, zucchini, bell peppers, onion)
  • garlic, minced
  • dried oregano or thyme
  • salt and pepper
  • fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Toss vegetables with 1–2 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper, and a pinch of herbs.
  2. Place chicken on a baking tray. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice and zest, garlic, and herbs. Brush over chicken.
  3. Scatter vegetables around the chicken on the tray. Roast for 25–30 minutes, until chicken is cooked through and veggies are tender.
  4. Turn vegetables halfway through cooking for even roasting.
  5. Let rest a few minutes, then drizzle with any pan juices. Garnish with parsley if desired.
  6. Serve warm with a side of crusty bread or a light grain like quinoa or rice.
