Korean Red Noodles

Home

Discription

Spicy Korean noodles blaze with gochujang, creating a fiery, flavorful sting that wakes the senses. Tangled noodles soak up the heat, offering a bold, comforting punch in every steaming bite.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • sauce
  • onion
  • red pepper
  • soya sauce
  • chillie sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Chop the onion and red pepper into thin slices.
  3. In a pan, sauté the onion and red pepper in a little oil until they start to soften.
  4. Add the sauce to the pan and cook for 1–2 minutes to bloom the flavors.
  5. Pour in a splash of soya sauce and a small amount of chilli sauce to taste. Stir to combine.
  6. Toss the cooked noodles into the pan with the sauce and vegetables. Stir well to coat evenly.
  7. Cook for another 1–2 minutes, adjusting seasoning with more sauce or soy if needed.
  8. Serve hot. Optionally garnish with extra chili flakes or sliced pepper.
